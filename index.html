07 â€” Risks & Constraints

What Could Go Wrong & Our Limits

Major Risks

  • Permit Delays

    Multiple authorizations required (campus occupation, health certificate, DDPP). Administrative delays can push back opening date.

  • Unreliable Suppliers

    Need regular deliveries of coffee, milk, and supplies. A supplier failure could halt operations completely.

  • Low Customer Demand

    If fewer than 100 customers per day, revenues won't cover costs. Success depends on effective marketing and location.

  • Staff Absence/Training

    Relying on student volunteers who have classes and exams. Staff absences or inadequate training could disrupt service.

  • Budget Overrun

    First-time project with tight budget. Costs can exceed estimates (equipment, permits, waste). No extra funding available.

Project Constraints

  • Time

    6-week pilot maximum. Must open by Week 3 to collect data before semester ends.

  • Budget

    Fixed budget of â‚¬10,500 with no possibility of extra funding. Must break even by Week 4.

  • Regulatory

    Must comply with food safety regulations (DDPP declaration, health certificate, HACCP training).

  • Resources

    Limited to 5 team members + 3-4 volunteers. Everyone has classes, exams, and other commitments.

  • Space

    Need central, visible campus location. Space must be approved by administration and accessible.

  • Infrastructure

    Requires 230V power, water access, and adequate furniture. Limited equipment options within budget.
